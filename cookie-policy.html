Cookie Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2025

The Entity Studios operates across the UK, EU, and India through its registered entities: The Entity Studios Private Limited (India), The-Entity Ltd (UK), and The Entity OÜ (Estonia) (hereinafter referred to as “The Entity Studios”). We are committed to respecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how cookies are used across our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us improve your browsing experience, remember preferences, and measure website traffic.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies only for essential functionality and analytics purposes. Specifically:

  • To ensure secure, stable performance of our website
  • To understand visitor behavior and optimize user experience via Google Analytics
  • To comply with legal and technical best practices

We do not use cookies to track personal identities, nor do we sell or share any visitor data.

3. Types of Cookies in Use

Essential Cookies

Required for website core functionality (e.g., page loading, session authentication).

Analytics Cookies

Used via Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior anonymously.

Functional Cookies

Used for remembering preferences like language or theme.

Note: We do not serve ads or use third-party advertising cookies on our site.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies directly from your browser settings. Please note, blocking essential cookies may affect the site’s usability.

5. Data Compliance & Security

We maintain compliance with:

  • GDPR (EU)
  • UK Data Protection Act
  • Indian IT & Privacy Guidelines

Our production systems follow ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standards and TPN Gold-aligned security practices. We do not store personal data via cookies and take all reasonable precautions to ensure anonymous data processing.

6. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in law, tech tools, or business practices. We encourage you to review this page regularly.

7. Contact

For questions or concerns, feel free to reach out:

Email: contact@entity.ltd

By continuing to browse our website, you consent to our limited and transparent use of cookies.
