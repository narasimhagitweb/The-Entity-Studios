The Studio at a Glance

The Entity Studios is a global game animation studio delivering production-ready pipelines for studios worldwide. Built by industry veterans, we combine world-class expertise in character setup, motion capture, and animation workflows with the operational maturity to support projects of any scale.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by industry veterans, The Entity Studios was created to blend the craft of high-end animation pipelines with scalable, secure global delivery operations. Our leadership team brings decades of experience working on some of the industry’s most iconic game titles. We exist to serve studios that require reliable, highly specialized animation support integrated directly into their production workflows.
