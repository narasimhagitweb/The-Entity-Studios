The Entity Studios

At a Glance

The Entity Studios is a global game animation studio delivering production-ready pipelines to developers worldwide.

Founded by industry veterans, we specialize in character setup, animation, and motion capture workflows,

combining deep craft with the operational discipline to support projects of any size or scope.

Our Story

Established in 2024, The Entity Studios was created to blend the precision of high-end animation pipelines
with the flexibility and security of global delivery. Our leadership team brings decades of experience
from some of the industry’s most iconic game titles. We partner with studios that demand reliable,
specialized animation teams integrated seamlessly into their production workflows.

Global
Operations

The Entity Studios operates as a globally registered company, with legal entities and delivery teams positioned to support studios across key regions.

Our distributed operations enable us to work seamlessly across time zones, providing reliable communication, secure content workflows, and production-ready delivery for every client.
